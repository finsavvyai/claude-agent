‚ö° Can I really build a full app in 2 hours?

Yes. Our users have built: SaaS platforms with Stripe payments (2.5 hours), mobile apps with authentication (1.8 hours), AI chatbots with RAG (45 minutes). Luna automates all the setup, configuration, and deployment - you focus on your unique features.

üí∞ What's the catch? This seems too good to be true.
